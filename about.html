About Africa Gold Exchange

Recognized Gold Mining Company in Cameroon

Founded in 2013 and officially registered in 2017, Africa Gold Exchange LLC is a leading gold trading platform based in Bamenda, North West Cameroon. Our journey began with a vision to connect local miners and gold traders with global markets, facilitating fair and transparent transactions. Over the years, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry, driven by our commitment to quality and ethical practices.

As a proud advocate for local artisans, we ensure that our operations contribute to the sustainable development of the communities we serve. Our team consists of experienced professionals dedicated to fostering a safe and efficient gold trading environment.

Our Mission

At Africa Gold Exchange LLC, our mission is to empower individuals and businesses by providing access to premium African gold resources while ensuring integrity and transparency in every transaction. We strive to be a catalyst for economic growth in Africa by supporting local miners and promoting responsible sourcing practices.

We envision a thriving marketplace where gold is not just a commodity but a means to enrich investors, uplift communities, and foster sustainable development. Our commitment to excellence drives us to continually improve our services and expand our reach, connecting Africa's rich resources with the world.
