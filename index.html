About Africa Gold Exchange

Overview

Founded in 2013 and officially registered in 2017, Africa Gold Exchange LLC is a leading gold trading platform based in Bamenda, North West Cameroon.

Our journey began with a vision to connect local miners and gold traders with global markets, facilitating fair and transparent transactions. Over the years, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry, driven by our commitment to quality and ethical practices.

As a proud advocate for local artisans, we ensure that our operations contribute to the sustainable development of the communities we serve.

Our commited team consists of experienced professionals dedicated to fostering a safe and efficient gold mining and trading environment.
